COMMISSIONS

I'm open to commissions! If you'd like custom artwork, character designs, or other creative work, I'd love to hear from you.

Commission Types

  • Character Designs - Custom character creation with full reference sheets
  • Illustrations - Single character or group artwork in various styles
  • Comic Panels - Multi-panel comic strips or sequences
  • Concept Art - Environmental designs and concept work
  • Animation Frames - Sprite sheets or animation sequences

Pricing

Pricing varies depending on complexity, style, and scope of the project. Below is a general guide:

Type Description Price Range
Sketch Simple pencil/digital sketch $15 - $30
Lineart Detailed line drawing, no color $25 - $50
Colored Illustration Full color single character $40 - $100
Character Sheet Multiple angles and expressions $80 - $200
Comic Page Full comic page (4-6 panels) $100 - $250

Commission Process

  1. Send me a commission inquiry with details about your project
  2. I'll provide you with a custom quote and timeline
  3. Once agreed, a 50% deposit is required to begin work
  4. I'll create sketches for your approval
  5. After approval, I'll continue to the final artwork
  6. Final payment due upon completion
  7. You receive the finished artwork in high resolution

Important Information

  • I accept multiple payment methods including PayPal and bank transfer
  • Commercial use requires additional licensing fees
  • Turnaround time typically ranges from 2-6 weeks depending on complexity
  • You'll receive work-in-progress updates throughout the commission
  • Revisions are included in the commission package
  • Final artwork will be high-resolution and suitable for print
