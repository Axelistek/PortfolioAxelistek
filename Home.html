CURRENT PROJECTS

BON BON'S GUIDE TO LOVE AND DEATH

A Visual Novel Adventure

Bon Bon's guide to love & death is a narrative driven visual novel centered on an exiled protagonist navigating relationships in a dying world. Exiled into a collapsing desert beneath towering moving cities, you wake up cursed, incomplete, and bound to a talking axe with questionable motives. The sky is counting down to an eclipse, the Carriers are on the move, and you have exactly fourteen days to decide what your life means before it ends. Each day you choose one character to spend time with, shaping relationships, uncovering secrets, and locking in consequences you cannot undo. You can form a full bond with only one soul, and trying to pursue everyone will lead to a guaranteed bad ending. With multiple endings ranging from romantic to tragic, Subterria blends time pressure, character driven storytelling, and dark humor into a visual novel about connection in a world that is already falling apart.

SUBTERRIA RISING

A Comic Series

This comic is a prologue to a larger science-fantasy narrative set in a desert world dominated by massive sentient carriers and scattered underground settlements. It follows Lova, a mercenary from a race believed to be extinct, during a self-contained job that takes place amid the Moon Harvest Festival. Tasked with hunting a legendary subterranean creature, the story presents everyday survival in the Subterria prior to the main events of the series. The project introduces the setting, social tensions, and the protagonist's role in the world without relying on knowledge of the broader plot.
