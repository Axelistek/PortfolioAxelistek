BON BONS GUIDE TO DEATH AND LOVE

A Dating Visual Novel

Gameplay Mechanics:

Timed day cycle (14 days until apocalypse): Each day you choose 1 character to spend time with.

Affection meter + decision impact: Choices unlock new scenes, endings, or death.

Multiple endings: Romantic, platonic, tragic, funny, or cursed

One guaranteed "bad end" if you try to romance everyone (Bon-Bon carves out your heart)

You start the game with only half a heart. Build relationships and earn Heart Points by connecting with characters across the world. If you earn enough, half of their heart will fuse with yours, completing your own.

You can only fully bond with one soul. Choose carefully.

Romanticable Characters

VANCE BLOODHOUND

Difficulty: ☆ ☆

Type: Underground Rockstar / Grief Baggage / Secret Softie

Loves: Cigarettes, riding fast, fighting injustice, his bandmates, loyalty

Route Mood: Deadpan teasing → mutual protection → emotional jailbreak

Vance isn't looking for love, he's looking for revenge. But there's something in the way you don't flinch around him. His route includes bar fights, a duet performance, and maybe… a hand on his scars he never lets anyone touch.

STEPH SPRINKLEFARTS

Difficulty: ☆

Type: Mute Songbird / Baby Sweetheart

Loves: Singing, Cockatoos, Starry nights, Drawing, Making bracelets for people, The band that saved her

Route Mood: Warm + Healing + Innocent

Steph's route is a musical bond. You'll earn her affection by learning her rhythms both literal and emotional. It's a gentle route full of shared silences, humming duets, and a final stage performance under the dying sky.

ADRIK "TORCH" VOLKOV

Difficulty: ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ (Hell Mode™)

Type: Repressed Dadcore / Desert Cryptid / Built like a tragedy

Loves: Quiet solitude, his adopted daughter Lova, old love letters, fighting for an end to the cycle of violence

Route Mood: Cold detachment → subtle protection → inevitable heartbreak

Torch doesn't want to be loved. In fact, he'll go out of his way to make sure you don't. He keeps people at a distance because he believes he's dangerous, and he's not wrong. Haunted by a parasitic twin, emotionally carved up by guilt, Torch hides in the desert not just to protect others, but to make sure no one ever tries to save him.

V1X3N BLAZECLAW

Difficulty: ☆ ☆

Type: Glam Punk Disaster / Attention Needer

Loves: Late-night jam sessions, raw honest music, (my head is exploding wait)

Route Mood: Party mess → heart-to-heart → self-worth

Vixen lives like the world's a stage. Loud, glittering, impossible to ignore. He plays the asshole sometimes, convinced people only want him for his money or his body, but that bravado hides someone insecure and a little too easy to fool. Behind the eyeliner and chaos is a kid terrified of being left alone. His route includes reckless nights, unguarded confessions, and the slow realization that he doesn't need an audience to matter.

LENOR CALLOWN

Difficulty: ☆☆☆☆☆

Type: Divine Executioner / Haunted Gunslinger

Loves: Quiet chapels at dawn, well-oiled revolvers, cigars

Route Mood: Relentless violence → wary alliance → smoldering, forbidden romance

Lenor is a man you should never stand too close to. Death follows wherever he goes. Balanced between prayer and bloodshed, he's feared by all, trusted by none. Yet in his silence lingers a hunger, a glance heavier than a bullet, firelight holding too long on his eyes. Love with Lenor isn't safe. It's a risk, temptation, and inevitable surrender.

BON-BON (GUIDE CHARACTER)

Bon-Bon is your unwanted spiritual advisor, former murder weapon, and now self-proclaimed "Love Consultant of the Wastes." He guides the player through the game, offers commentary (mostly unhelpful), and occasionally unlocks hidden lore….often by accident.

Personality

Sarcastic, dramatic, emotionally avoidant
Obsessed with being perceived as "cool" despite being a talking axe

In-Game Role

Narrator and guide
Unlocks Lova's route if bonded with
May lie, mislead, or emotionally blackmail the player for fun

The truth? Probably…

Bon-Bon was manifested

Lova spent years projecting all her unspoken pain, unresolved grief, and deeply repressed affection into her weapon. The axe never talked back... until one day it did.

Maybe it was her faith. Maybe it was a curse. Maybe emotions really do have weight, and hers finally tipped the scale. Whatever the reason, Bon-Bon became sentient during a battle she never got to finish. And then she vanished. No goodbye. Just gone.

"She left me. Left without a word, without even turning around. I was there…carved from everything she refused to feel. And when it all got too heavy, she dropped me like a weight she didn't want to carry anymore."

Now Bon-Bon is stuck with you. And here's the kicker…he's not doing this to help you. He's doing it out of pure, spite-driven curiosity.

"See, turns out, when you're made of someone else's emotional repression, you don't get a lot of closure. And when that someone ghosts you without so much as a 'thanks for carrying my trauma,' you either shrivel up in the sand... or reinvent yourself."

So Bon-Bon reinvented himself as the ultimate petty act of rebellion: A love guide
